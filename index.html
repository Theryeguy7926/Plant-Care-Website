Welcome to The Green Thumb!

What is this all about?

I am a student at ASU and this is a school project so for my CIS 300 class! We have to build a website based on something that we are interested in. I decided to make my website about plants. Something that I enjoy doing most mornings is caring for my plants that I have.

What kind of information can I find here?

I have created several pages about caring for houselpants. Each page covers a topic of care for the plants in your home. You can access these pages on the navigation bar.

Why should I be interested in houseplants?

Houseplants are the easiest living thing to take care of that isnt an animal or a baby. Not only is it a great stepping stone to caring for those, it is extremely rewarding. Personally, I love to watch plants grow and see them thriving in my home. They also make my living space look so much more alive and add a pop of color.
